Professional Biography

I'm Tony Waterfield, I graduated from Virginia Commonwealth University in 1987 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Information Systems from the School of Business.

My career in Information Technology began immediatley after graduation working for a major local company. Over a 20 year career in IT support and development I had the opportunity to work in many buiness areas.

These areas include Sales and Trade Marketing, Manufacturing and Plant Engineering. I have worked as Lead Technology Analysts guiding a team of contractors developing electronic forms capturing legal sales contracts and marketing programs.

These forms were stored and archived in a document repository and data was extracted and stored in a database for reporting and data feeds to Marketing applications.
